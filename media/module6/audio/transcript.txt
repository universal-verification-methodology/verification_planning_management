[Slide 16] Next section: Verification & testing methods.

[Slide 17] 1. Protocol rule testing. Each protocol rule maps to checker fires, coverpoints, and at least one directed or random test. Illegal sequences and corner timing documented with expected checker behavior. Refer to the diagram on the right.

[Slide 18] 2. Multi-agent scenario testing. Virtual sequences stress cross-agent ordering, arbitration, and backpressure. System scoreboard validates end-to-end data and sideband signals.

[Slide 19] 3. Maintainability reviews. Architecture review before adding agents — avoid duplicate monitors and conflicting checkers. ./scripts/module6.sh --check validates multi-agent and protocol planning artifacts.
