[Slide 45] Assessment checklist Can explain verification’s role and goals in the SV/UVM context. Has produced a written verification scope (in-scope, out-of-scope, assumptions). Has a concise verification strategy document for the chosen DUT. Has an initial test catalogue with traceability to requirements. Has a high-level coverage plan (what to cover and why). Can articulate a basic regression and sign-off concept, even if details come later.
